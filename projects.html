San Francisco Chinatown Recreation

2020 - 2021

Due to the pandemic, most activities were forced to shut down. San Francisco' Chinatown Alleyway Tours decided to take matters into their own hands and host tours online through zoom and inviting attendees to a private world to continue giving tours during this period. My position in this project was lead builder where I would take buildings from the real world and recreate San Francisco's Chinatown in Minecraft.
