San Francisco Chinatown Recreation

2020 - 2021

[ CNET ] [Minecraft Education ]

Due to the pandemic, most activities were forced to shut down. San Francisco' Chinatown Alleyway Tours decided to take matters into their own hands and host tours online through zoom and inviting attendees to a private world to continue giving tours during this period. My position in this project was lead builder where I would take buildings from the real world and recreate San Francisco's Chinatown in Minecraft.

The recreation was possible thanks to tools such as Google Maps, people on sight that was able to take pictures of angles that were hard to see, and plugins such as World Edit. Most people in the Minecraft community would agree that one Minecraft block would be equivilant to roughly three feet, so that was the measurement we went with when building. Google Maps has a useful tool called "Measure Distance" so when we used that tool, we could accurately see the dimensions of each building.
